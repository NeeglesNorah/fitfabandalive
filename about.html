About Queen Iwo

QueenIwo Akinyoyenu

Life | Executive | Holistic Wellness Coach

QueenIwo is a globally certified Holistic Wellness Coach and the founder of Fit Fab and Alive™, a transformative wellness movement designed to equip purpose-driven professionals and business owners with the tools to live whole—spirit, soul, and body.

With over 25 years of experience and more than 15,000 lives impacted across 10+ countries, QueenIwo empowers leaders, professionals, and business owners to shift from burnout to balance, stress to strength, and from survival-mode to full-thrive through her faith-informed and science-based approach to holistic wellness and purposeful living.

QueenIwo is a dynamic speaker, corporate wellness trainer, and digital content creator, helping individuals and organizations integrate wellbeing into their lifestyle, leadership, and legacy. Whether she's delivering a keynote on work-life harmony, leading a transformation challenge, or creating inspiring content for YouTube, QueenIwo's message is clear: You can live whole, walk in purpose, and show up with vitality—every day.
